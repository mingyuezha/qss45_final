Policy Recommendations

Local Relevance of Health Guidance

Health information should reflect local healthcare systems, regulations, treatment availability, and country-specific public health guidance.

02

Improving Source Quality

Heavy reliance on non-institutional sources such as YouTube, Facebook, and other user-generated content platforms raises concerns about information quality and reliability.

Transparency and Accountability

Platforms should clearly disclose how and why AI-generated health responses are amplified, suppressed, or withheld across different health categories.

Key Findings
🌞
Baidu favors TCM queries
Baidu returns AI overviews for 63.3% of Traditional Chinese Medicine queries vs only 41.4% on Google US — a 21.9 percentage point gap reflecting platform alignment with culturally dominant medicine.
⚖
Google US leads on legally variable topics
Google US returns AI overviews for 58.6% of legally variable health topics (e.g. abortion, gender-affirming care) vs Baidu's 41.4% — suggesting Baidu suppresses politically sensitive content more aggressively.
